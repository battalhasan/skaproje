Canteen Challenge

The meeting with the canteen manager, Mr. Kamil, was not very constructive. Mr. Kamil crossed his arms and pretended to listen politely to the Eco Team, but his body language clearly showed he didn't care about what was being said.

Mr. Kamil: "Look kids, I'm running a business here. If I reduce portions, students won't feel full. If I use paper instead of plastic for packaging, my costs go up and sales go down. Besides, it’s my money going into the trash, why does it bother you?"
Mert: "It's not just your money going into the trash, Mr. Kamil; it's our future. And we will certainly take action for our future."
