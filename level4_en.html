The Great Crisis

Sponsors found with the support of the Parent-Teacher Association helped both in the installation of smart systems and the creation of the permaculture garden. While everything was going well, news from the municipality came as a shock: the green area adjacent to the school was put up for sale for a private parking lot project. The Eco Team's hard work was in jeopardy.

Mert: "Okay, there is a parking problem in our neighborhood, but I still can't accept that all our hard work is being sacrificed for a pile of concrete."
Zeynep: "No giving up, friends! We will either start a legal process or run a campaign on social media with public support."
